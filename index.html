About Me

I have had the opportunity to work as a product analyst and specialist in the research and development department of an industrial manufacturing company, where I gained valuable experience analyzing and evaluating products, as well as developing and implementing new products. Through this role, I gained a deep understanding of the product development process and learned to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams.

With over six years of experience, I have designed, developed, debugged, and tested various embedded system applications, specializing in bare-metal embedded design and wireless technologies such as LORA, NB-IoT, and Bluetooth. Throughout my career, I have participated in the development of more than four products in the embedded utilities area.

I have a certificate in Introduction to Self-Driving Cars, which has allowed me to gain knowledge and skills in image processing using programming languages such as Python and C++ and frameworks like OpenCV.

Additionally, I have gained over a year of experience as a DevOps in a web network management application, allowing me to hone my skills in both back-end and front-end development. Through this experience, I gained valuable insights into the software development lifecycle and learned to work effectively in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

As a freelancer, I developed a front-end application utilizing pure JavaScript and JQuery for image processing, gaining experience in web technologies such as React, HTML, and CSS. My experience also includes data analysis and browser test automation, further strengthening my proficiency in software development. I am committed to staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in web technologies and continuously improving my skills as a developer.

I invite you to visit my LinkedIn and GitHub pages for further details. On these platforms, you can find a collection of projects that showcase my skills and experience in software development. I am confident that these projects will provide further insight into my capabilities and passion for creating innovative solutions.
